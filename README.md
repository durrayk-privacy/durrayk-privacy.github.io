# Privacy Architecture & AI Governance Portfolio

This repository hosts the live technical portfolio for **Durray Kausar**, specialized in Privacy Architecture, Core Governance Framework Design, and Technical Engineering Controls.

## 🛠️ Key Architectural Implementations Documented
* **4-Tier Access Control System:** Zero-trust architecture tracking hardware MFA requirements and session-recording parameters for high-sensitivity datasets.
* **Data Minimization Pipelines:** End-to-end processing models utilizing pseudonymization, automated redaction, and strict legal-hold retention automation.
* **EU AI Act Alignment Matrices:** Programmatic classification mapping tool deployments to proper impact assessment paths and human-in-the-loop review gates.
* **Third-Party Risk Engineering:** Custom risk-scoring matrices and automated DPA requirements banning systemic re-indexing by biometric scrapers.

## ⚖️ Regulatory Framework Coverage
Architected frameworks map technical and operational controls to:
* **SCA & ECPA** (Stored Communications & Electronic Communications Privacy Acts)
* **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection Rule)
* **GDPR & CPRA** (General Data Protection Regulation / California Privacy Rights Act)
* **HIPAA & FERPA** (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability / Family Educational Rights and Privacy Acts)
