Havenwatch Foundation

2026: Data protection framework for child safety case systems. Designed 4-tier access architecture, data minimization & pseudonymization pipelines, and retention schedules across SCA, COPPA, ECPA, GDPR, and HIPAA compliance domains. Zero control failures before go-live.

—

Access & authentication

4-tier architecture: Tier 4 (minor records) requires hardware token MFA, session recording, real-time access alerts. Tier 1–3 enforce least-privilege, device compliance, 90-day access reviews.

—

Data minimization & retention

Classification at intake, pseudonymization of minor identifiers, automated redaction, regulatory-aligned retention schedules with auto-expiry flags and dual-sign-off legal holds.

Regulatory alignment

EU AI Act risk tiers

Unacceptable

Social scoring · real-time biometric surveillance

Blocked from deployment regardless of vendor pressure or business case.

High Risk

AI grading · behavioral prediction · automated admissions

Full DPIA required · human oversight documented · annual review mandated.

Limited Risk

AI chatbots · content recommendation engines

Transparency notice required · users informed · logged in AI inventory.

Minimal Risk

Spell check · scheduling · non-personalized analytics

Noted in AI inventory · no additional controls required.
