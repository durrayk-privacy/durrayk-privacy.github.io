Executive Summary

This portfolio documents operational privacy architectures, technical governance frameworks, and data minimization pipelines engineered to secure sensitive data assets across complex regulatory environments. Focus domains include the Stored Communications Act (SCA), COPPA, ECPA, GDPR, and HIPAA.

01. Access Control Architecture (4-Tier Model)

Designed and validated tiered access controls governing high-stakes datasets to enforce strict zero-trust operational parameters.

Tier 4 - Minor Records (Highest Risk)

  • Hardware token MFA mandates paired with real-time anomalous access alerts and automated session recording.
  • Just-In-Time (JIT) access authorization active for live case processing only; immediate credential revocation executes upon role updates.
  • Enforced mandatory quarterly access verification reviews.

Tiers 1-3 - Case-Related Data

  • Software-based MFA tokens required alongside contextual device compliance posture checks.
  • Enforced least-privilege role assignments with comprehensive audit logging of all system touches.

Result: 100% control coverage achieved pre-launch; zero unauthorized access failures recorded across development and production testing pipelines.

02. Data Minimization & Retention Pipelines

Operationalized programmatic data pipelines ensuring structural compliance from intake through deletion lifecycles.

  • At Intake: Data injection parameters are strictly constrained to necessary transactional fields mapped directly to documented legal bases.
  • In Transit & At Rest: Core identifiers are dynamically pseudonymized (e.g., 'Minor_ID_00127'), with automated PII redaction rules executed prior to UI presentation layer rendering. Cryptographic protections utilize AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.
  • At Expiration: Automated expiry flags trigger programmatic deletions on retention schedules. Overriding legal holds require verified dual-authorized sign-offs.

03. Vendor Risk Assessment & DPA Framework

Built a custom risk-scoring compliance matrix deployed to audit third-party services handling highly sensitive operational metrics. Successfully evaluated 15+ specialized vendor systems, deploying 8 platforms under green clear criteria and rejecting non-negotiable compliance gaps.

DPA Architecture Mandates

  • Algorithmic Transparency: Binding disclosures covering underlying training datasets and demographic bias testing results categorized by race, age, and gender.
  • Technical Guardrails: Contractual and engineering blocks preventing the continuous automated re-indexing or web-crawling of deleted data profiles.
  • Downstream Enforcement: Sub-processor notification boundaries capped at 5-10 days paired with mandatory annual SOC 2 Type II audit verifications.

04. AI Governance & EU AI Act Risk Framework

Constructed an enterprise AI governance framework classifying, cataloging, and mitigating pipeline deployments against established regulatory risk models.

Risk Level Examples Operational Control Parameters
